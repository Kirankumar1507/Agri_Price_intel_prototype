The Problem

Farmers lose ₹thousands/quintal selling at the nearest mandi instead of the best one

📍

Nearest ≠ Best

Farmers default to the closest APMC mandi. But a mandi 40km away might pay ₹300/qtl more — even after transport costs.

📊

Data Exists, Access Doesn't

data.gov.in publishes daily mandi prices. But no farmer checks a government API before loading their truck.

🧮

Nobody Does the Math

Modal price − ₹5/km transport = net price. This simple subtraction isn't done for the farmer anywhere.

🗣️

Language Barrier

Government scheme documents are in bureaucratic English. Farmers need explanations in their language.

The Solution

One search. One answer. In your language.
